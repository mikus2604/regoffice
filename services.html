Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a registered office address?

Yes, every UK limited company, LLP, and PLC must have a registered office address in the country where it's registered (England & Wales, Scotland, or Northern Ireland). This address appears on the public Companies House register.

Can I use a home address?

Legally yes, but it means your home address becomes publicly available on Companies House. Many directors prefer using a professional service address to protect their privacy and maintain a corporate image.

How quickly can you set up my service?

Most services are activated within 24 hours of completing our verification process. In many cases, we can provide same-day setup for urgent requirements.

What happens to my mail?

We receive all mail sent to your registered or service address. Important documents are scanned and sent to you digitally. Physical mail can be forwarded to your preferred address at intervals you choose, or held securely for collection.

Is this legal and compliant?

Absolutely. Using a professional registered office or directors' service address is fully compliant with UK company law and Companies House regulations. We maintain all necessary authorisations and comply with AML requirements.

Can I change my registered office address later?

Yes, you can change your registered office address at any time by filing the appropriate forms with Companies House. If you're already our client, we can assist with this process.
