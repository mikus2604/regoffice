Pricing FAQs

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Annual services are billed yearly; monthly services are billed on your chosen date each month.

Can I change services later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or add services at any time. We'll pro-rata any payments to ensure fairness.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual services. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full.

What happens when I cancel?

Your service continues until the end of your paid period. We'll help you transition to a new address if required, and provide all necessary documentation for Companies House.
