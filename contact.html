Common Questions

How quickly can I get started?

Most services are activated within 24 hours. Once we receive your details and verify your identity (AML compliance), we'll confirm your address and you can start using it immediately.

What information do you need from me?

We require basic company information (or intended company name), director details, proof of identity (passport/driving licence), and proof of address. This is for AML compliance and Companies House requirements.

Do you offer same-day setup?

Yes, for urgent requirements we can often activate services the same day. Contact us directly by phone or mark your enquiry as urgent and we'll prioritise your setup.

Can I visit your office?

Yes, visits are welcome by appointment. Please contact us in advance to arrange a suitable time. Our address is 309 High Street, West Midlands, B70 8LX.
