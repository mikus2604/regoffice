Professional Address Services Built on Trust, Compliance, and Transparency

JMAM Services Ltd was founded to solve a simple problem: UK businesses need compliant, professional addresses without the cost and complexity of traditional office space.

Based at our West Midlands location, we specialise in registered office addresses, directors' service addresses, and mail forwarding — providing the foundations every UK company requires to operate legally and professionally.

Our Approach

We believe business administration should be straightforward. That's why we've stripped away unnecessary complexity, focusing on three core principles:

Compliance First

Every service meets current Companies House and company law requirements. We monitor regulatory changes and update our processes accordingly, so your business stays compliant without constant oversight.

Security & Privacy

Your correspondence and data are handled with institutional-grade security. All staff are trained in GDPR compliance, anti-money laundering procedures, and confidentiality protocols. Physical mail is stored securely; digital scans use encrypted transmission.

Transparency & Support

No hidden fees, no confusing contract terms, no automated systems that leave you stranded. Our UK-based team provides clear guidance, answers questions promptly, and treats every client with professionalism and respect.

Your reputation matters. When clients, suppliers, or banks see your registered address, it reflects on your entire business. That's why we maintain a professional location, handle correspondence with care, and respond to queries within hours — not days.

We're here to support your growth, protect your privacy, and ensure you meet every legal requirement with confidence.
