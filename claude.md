# Registered Office Address Service Website

## Project Overview
A professional, SEO-optimized website for a UK-based registered office address service business. The website showcases services, builds trust, and converts visitors into customers.

## Business Context
- **Industry**: Business Services / Virtual Office Services
- **Location**: United Kingdom
- **Target Audience**:
  - Startups and entrepreneurs
  - Small to medium businesses
  - Remote/international companies needing UK presence
  - Limited companies requiring Companies House compliance

## Core Services
1. Registered Office Address provision
2. Mail handling and forwarding
3. Business address services
4. Companies House compliance support
5. Professional business presence

## Website Goals
1. Generate qualified leads through contact forms
2. Establish credibility and trust
3. Rank well for UK registered office search terms
4. Provide clear pricing and service information
5. Answer common customer questions
6. Enable easy online inquiry/signup process

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Modern HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (vanilla or React/Next.js)
- **Styling**: Responsive CSS with mobile-first approach
- **SEO**: Schema.org markup, semantic HTML, optimized meta tags
- **Performance**: Fast loading, optimized images, minimal dependencies
- **Compliance**: GDPR-compliant contact forms, accessibility (WCAG 2.1)

## SEO Strategy

### Target Keywords (UK-focused)
- Primary: "registered office address UK"
- Secondary: "registered office service", "virtual office UK", "business address service"
- Long-tail: "cheap registered office address", "London registered office", "Companies House registered office"

### On-Page SEO Elements
- Optimized title tags (60 chars max)
- Meta descriptions (155 chars max)
- H1-H6 heading hierarchy
- Alt text for all images
- Internal linking structure
- Schema markup (LocalBusiness, Organization, Service)
- Fast page load times (<3s)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clean URL structure

### Content Strategy
- Location-specific landing pages (London, Manchester, Birmingham, etc.)
- Blog section for SEO content:
  - "How to register a company in the UK"
  - "Do I need a registered office address?"
  - "Registered office vs trading address"
  - "Companies House compliance guide"

## Design Principles
1. **Professional & Trustworthy**: Clean, corporate design with blue/grey color scheme

3. **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive across all devices
4. **Fast Loading**: Optimized images, minimal JavaScript
5. **Accessible**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
6. **Conversion-Focused**: Strategic placement of contact forms and phone numbers
